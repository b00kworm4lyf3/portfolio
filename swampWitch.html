Swamp Witch

SFX Makeup Character Design
Completed: December 2018

Overview

I created the swamp witch as my final project for the Master Makeup Artist program at The Art of Makeup in Portland, Oregon. She is a culmination of all the skills we learned in the SFX section of the program under the guidance of Thomas Surprenant, which included lifecasting models, character design, sculpting, molding, and casting in different materials, and the application and painting of prosthetic makeup pieces on my model Lindsey.

Development Process

Research & Planning

When planning this project I wanted to make something that showed I could be creative, but also make something realistic and screen-ready. A witch seemed to be a good base character to show these skills, and is a relatively clean slate to work with when building a character alongside the makeup. Sadly my Pinterest inspo board for this project has been lost to time, or I would link it here to show a bit more of my thought process.

Lifecasting and Sculpting

The first step in creating a makeup look like this is lifecasting your model. You can sculpt on a generic face, but if you have the opportunity to make something that fits perfectly you should do it.
